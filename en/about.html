About Us

We established Ray Investment Company to be one of the gates to the growth of the economic environment in the field of venture capital through participation in the capital and the support of technical and management expertise to develop entrepreneurial and emerging projects. We seek and support companies to grow through early stage investment. In Ray Investment, we also seek to be one of the main elements for developing the entrepreneurship system and achieving diversity in investment opportunities. We are investing in multiple rounds at an early stage with high growth potential for tech startups. In addition to our extensive network of contacts to add value to our investors through our ability to open doors and guide them.
